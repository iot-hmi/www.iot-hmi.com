Immense Oak Technologies Signs Distribution Agreement with Kanaue Applied Materials Corp.

Dec 6, 2021

Hsinchu, Taiwan – Immense Oak Technologies, Inc. (IOT) and Kanaue Applied Materials Corp. (Kanaue) entered a distribution agreement that allows Kanaue to distribute IOT’s products in Taiwan.

Kanaue, ever since its inception, has been serving semi-conductor foundries, LCD manufacturers and other high-tech manufacturers in Taiwan. Kanaue has established itself as a premier distributor/supplier for sensors, lighting sources for photolithography equipment, static and particles removal equipment, and chemicals. In addition, Kanaue offers services for enhancing the manufacturing equipment for better yield rates and shorter down time. Kanaue believes that working with IOT, Kanaue can elevate the product and service offering to another level. Kanaue is committed to bring IOT’s products and services to the equipment vendors, especially for the semiconductor and LCD foundries. In addition, Kanaue is working with IOT for sensor visualization and connectivity to the cloud. Kanaue combines its sensor product lines with IOT’s technology for offering innovative products that can further enhance its customers productivity.
