Immense Oak Technologies Signs Distribution Agreement with 3ASCREEN Corporation

Sep 14, 2021

Hsinchu, Taiwan – Immense Oak Technologies, Inc. (IOT) today signed the distribution agreement with 3ASCREEN Corporation (3ASCREEN) for representing IOT and distributing IOT’s Ubique Panel product line. Under this new agreement, 3ACREEN becomes a distributor for serving customers in Taiwan. The agreement is effective immediately.

3ASCREEN, with over 10 years of experience serving customers in Taiwan, have fostered a strong reputation for the quality of its service and products. 3ASCREEN offer mid-to-large sized visual solutions to their customers. In the small-to-mid range of product offerings, 3ASCREEN want to offer IOT’s Ubique Panel to their customers. “With IOT’s products, we are able to complete our product line for serving a larger set of customers,” said Jeremy Yang, Director for Marketing & Product Integration Division at 3ACREEN. “With IOT’s products, technology and service, we are able to deliver solutions for fulfilling all customer specific requirements.”

“We are excited about the prospect of the business relationship with 3ASCREEN,” said Anthony Guo, President of IOT. “Not only can IOT provide products to 3ASCREEN’s customers, but IOT can also team up with 3ASCREEN to provide solutions to customers in smart building management, medical equipment, and industrial control segments.”

About 3ASCREEN

3ASCREEN is one of the most professional LCD supplier and total solution provider in TFT industry. The main products include TFT LCD, driving boards, High Brightness LCD, TP and bonding service. Its main resources are BOE and DMC. 3ASCREEN provide tailor made service, fulfilling all customer specific requirements. 3ASCREEN thrive to offer customers with total solution services. For more information, please visit www.3ascreen.com for more information.

About IOT

Immense Oak Technologies, Inc. is a leading solution provider for high performance Human-Machine Interface. Our solutions facilitate effective and efficient communications between humans and machines and between machines. Headquartered in Hsinchu, Taiwan, IOT thrives to serve our customers globally.
