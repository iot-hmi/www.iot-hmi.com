Immense Oak Technologies Signed a Distributon Agreement with STC.

Jul 27, 2023

Hsinchu, Taiwan – Immense Oak Technologies, Inc. (IOT), announced that it signed a distribution agreement with Sun Trading Company Limited STC for promoting IOT products and services in Japan.

STC has been a premier display device manufacturer and distributor for 44 years since its inception. STC provides customers with liquid crystal cells, board patterning, touch panels, optical connectors, and other products. STC has accumulated its reputation for quality and has acquired a large set of customers in Japan. These customers come from a wide range of industries. STC believes that IOT’s Smart HMI solutions can benefit STC’s customers as Smart HMI delivers excellent visual effects, significantly shortens development time, and lowers the overall system costs. After signing this distribution agreement, STC will work with IOT to offer quick and best services in the Japan market for promoting IOT’s products.

This distribution agreement takes effect immediately. IOT looks forward to a fruitful and rewarding business relationship with STC.
