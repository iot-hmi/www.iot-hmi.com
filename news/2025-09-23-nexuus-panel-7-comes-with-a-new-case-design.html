Immense Oak Technologies, Inc., Nexus Panel 7.0" Comes with a New Case Design

Sep 23, 2025

Hsinchu, Taiwan – Immense Oak Technologies, Inc. (IOT) rolls out a new case design for Nexus Panel 7.0”.

More and more customers demand IOT to offer a complete HMI hardware solution – an HMI with a case. We design this case in responding to the requests.

The design focuses on easy-to-adopt. The communication ports are easy to access, so are I/O pins. The VESA mount is on the back of the case for easy attachments. The assembly of the case along with the Nexus Panel is simple for cutting down the production time.

The customers can order our Nexus Panel 7.0” along with this case. On the other hand, we offer the design file for the customers to customize or manufacture the case for Nexus Panel.

Please visit applications for more details. Please contact us or our local distributors for more information.
