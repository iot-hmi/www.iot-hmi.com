Immense Oak Technologies Signed a strategic partnership MOU with Digihua Intelligent Systems Co., Ltd.

Dec 19, 2022

December 2022, Taichung and Hsinchu, Taiwan – Digihua Intelligent Systems Co., Ltd. (Digihua) and Immense Oak Technologies, Inc. (IOT) enters a strategic partnership by signing the MOU effective immediately.

Digihua is a premier Manufacturing Operations Management (MOM) system provider in Greater China and Southeastern Asia. MOM integrates Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP), Manufacturing Execution System (ERP), Quality Management System (QMS), and Industrial Internet of Things (IIOT) to provide solutions for Smart Manufacturing. The transformation from traditional factories to smart ones relies on the convergence of information technology (IT) and operation technology (OT). The backbone of the transformation is the edge systems. IOT’s HMI solutions will become the edge system in Digihua’ solution offerings.

IOT’s HMI can easily perform data collections from sensors or machineries. The data can be easily visualized in IOT’s solutions. Data visualization and threshold settings can be easily set and done. Furthermore, IOT HMI’s solution can easily transfer the data via OPC UA or MQTT to ERP or MES systems for further analysis and decision-making. IOT’s HMI solutions can easily install intelligence on the premises for making manufacturing processes smarter and more efficient.

Digihua and IOT will engage with the customers in a wide range of industries. Both Digihua and IOT are looking forward to a very rewarding relationship moving forward.
