Immense Oak Technologies Completed HMI Solutions with both RTP and CTP

Feb 11, 2022

Hsinchu, Taiwan – Immense Oak Technologies, Inc. (IOT) announced complete HMI solutions with both CTP and RTP for 4.3”, 5.0” and 7.0” LCM.

IOT is fully committed to provide a comprehensive HMI solution portfolio for addressing various application requirements. From the touch control perspective, IOT supports both RTP and CTP. From the LCM size point of view, we offer common 4.3”, 5.0” and 7.0” products. In addition, IOT offers ultra-wide temperature products. These products complete our portfolio for the mid-range HMI. IOT provides extension boards for adding RS-485, RS-232, CAN Bus, WiFi, Ethernet or other communication interfaces.

The entire product portfolio works with IOT’s Acorn Development Environment (ADE). ADE is the HMI development platform that can be downloaded from IOT’s website: Download - Immense Oak Technologies (iot-hmi.com). ADE is a low-code/no-code HMI development platform. Users can design HMI with fascinating visual effect without even writing a single line of code.

Please visit our product website: Product - Ubique Panel - Immense Oak Technologies (iot-hmi.com) for more details. These two products along with their starter kits are available immediately. Please contact our distributors or (sales@iot-hmi.com) for ordering.
