Immense Oak Technologies Participate in “Taipei International Electronic Show” (TAITRONICS) from October 26 to 28, 2022

Oct 7, 2022

Hsinchu, Taiwan – Immense Oak Technologies, Inc. (IOT) will participate in “Taipei International Electronic Show” (TAITRONICS) from October 26 to 28, 2022 in Taipei Nangang Exhibition Center, Hall 1 (TaiNEX1).

IOT will proudly present our latest products from both Ubique Panel and Magnus Panel product lines. Visitors will see how versatile and capable our products can be for delivering advanced applications. In addition, IOT will showcase ADE (Acorn Development Environment) on how easy users can design sophisticated GUI with fascinating visual effects from their HMI project in a very short time.

Come and visit our booth at J1109a. Talk to us and you’ll expand your horizon in your HMI design.

Please visit TAITRONICS official site for the show’s details.
