Immense Oak Technologies Launches AIOT Solution

Jul 8, 2022

Hsinchu, Taiwan – Immense Oak Technologies, Inc. (IOT) launches AIOT solution that targets the smart factory applications. The solution has three major parts:

Smart Sensor: IOT offers both wired and wireless sensors. These sensors are to gather information for noise, vibration, light, current, temperature, humidity, and particles. The reason for offering wireless sensors is for flexible installation.

Sensor Hub: Sensor Hub is to connect to all the installed sensors and uses SECSII and CIM for connecting to the cutomer’s IT systems. Sensor Hub offers data visualization for better human consumption. In addition, users can set threshold alarms with easy and use the alarms to control the hub’s I/O functions. Sensor Hub comes with a no-code development environment for quick deployment.

AI Server: The brain of the AIOT solution is AI Server. In AI Server, IOT offers multivariate time series data analysis with the data gathered from our sensors. IOT offers no-code AI model building. These models can serve for trend prediction, anomaly detection and failure prediction. IOT even offer the custom service for building customized AI models.

Please visit Technology - AIOT - Immense Oak Technologies to learn more about our offering.

AIOT is a service intensive product line. AIOT is committed to work with the customers to design the AIOT product/service they need. IOT provides technical consultancy, customized AIOT planning, AIOT product deployment and AI model building services.
