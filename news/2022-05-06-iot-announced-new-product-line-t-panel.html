Immense Oak Technologies Announced a New Product Line – T Panel

May 6, 2022

Hsinchu, Taiwan – Immense Oak Technologies, Inc. (IOT) proudly presented a brand new product line – T Panel.

T Panel product line is for advanced C++ developers who can take advantage of ST Micro’s free development platform – TouchGFX. TouchGFX is an advanced HMI/GUI development platform for STM32-based hardware. Developers, who are well versed in C++, can develop HMI with fascinating visual effect.

What T Panel offers: T Panel is a price competitive solution for developers who seek for a stable and yet powerful hardware platform with a LCM to develop their HMI project. IOT’s solution is based on STM32 MCU running at 480Mhz with 32MB DRAM and 2Gb flash memory for storage. In addition, T Panel offers 32-bit ARGB pixel color format that brings fascinating visual effects to the HMI designs. T Panel is versatile enough for a wide range of applications.

I/O Capabilities: IOT understands that I/O capabilities are critical for an HMI. T Panel offers GPIO, PWM, ADC, Input capture mode. UART is available by default. By working with IOT’s extension boards, T Panel can support Ethernet, WiFi, RS-232, RS-485 and audio line-out. Furthermore, IOT offers peripheral configuration source code for a quick start.

Comprehensive offerings: T Panel comes with 4.3”, 5.0”, and 7.0” LCM with RTP and CTP. These offerings can address a wide range of product development. For LCM beyond our current offerings, please contact us. We do offer custom-designed LCM.

Please visit our technology page Product - T Panel - Immense Oak Technologies for more details. For offerings, please visit Technology - T Panel - Immense Oak Technologies. Customized LCMs are available upon request. Please contact our local distributors or sales@iot-hmi.com for product inquiry.

IOT is fully committed to bring more HMI solutions to the market. Magnus Panel is our latest offering to the market. IOT will continue its effort to offer HMI solutions for the growing needs of customers.
